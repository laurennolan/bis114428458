Coeliac Disease - Eating Out Card

I have Coeliac Disease and cannot eat food or drink that contains gluten. I must stick to a gluten-free diet. I cannot eat Wheat, Barley, Oats, Rye and Spelt. Gluten is also used in many processed foods.

Please check ingredients for:

  • Noodles
  • Couscous
  • Soya Sauce
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Pasta
  • Beers, Stouts, Lagers
  • Baking Powder
  • Farina
  • Semolina
  • Malt

I can't eat any foods that have been deep fried in or flavoured with any ingredients containing gluten, e.g. battered fish and chips or gravies.

Please ensure my food does not come into contact with any food containing gluten or utensils or work surfaces that have been used to prepare meals with gluten.
